## Idea A — Industrial Brutalist Precision

**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalism meets Industrial Modernism

**Core Principles:**
1. Raw material honesty — concrete textures, exposed structure
2. High-contrast type hierarchy with editorial weight
3. Asymmetric grid with deliberate tension
4. Monochromatic base with a single bold accent

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: near-white concrete (#F5F4F2) with subtle grain texture
- Foreground: deep charcoal (#1A1A1A)
- Accent: construction orange (#E85D04) — evokes safety, action, energy
- Secondary: warm mid-gray (#8C8C8C)
Emotional intent: authority, durability, craftsmanship

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Offset hero with large type overlapping a full-bleed photo
- Services section using a horizontal scroll strip
- Stats bar with large numerals (12 years, 107+ projects, Top 10%)
- Contact section with a diagonal split

**Signature Elements:**
1. Concrete texture overlay on hero (CSS noise + grain)
2. Bold section numbers (01, 02, 03) as structural anchors
3. Thick left-border accent lines on headings

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover reveals on service cards (slide-up overlay)
- Scroll-triggered entrance animations (stagger fade-up)
- CTA button with a fill-sweep animation on hover

**Animation:**
- Entrance: elements fade and translate-Y(20px) → 0 on scroll
- Section transitions: staggered children with 80ms delay
- Hover: cards lift with shadow deepening

**Typography System:**
- Display: Barlow Condensed (700) — industrial, bold
- Body: Source Serif 4 (400/600) — readable, professional
- Accent labels: Barlow (500, uppercase, tracked)
</text>

## Idea B — South Florida Coastal Authority

**Design Movement:** Coastal Modernism + Corporate Precision

**Core Principles:**
1. Clean, airy layouts evoking South Florida's open skies
2. Professional authority through structured typography
3. Photography-forward with generous white space
4. Trust signals prominently integrated (license, years, score)

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: crisp white (#FFFFFF) and light warm gray (#F8F7F5)
- Primary: deep navy (#0D2B45) — professionalism, trust
- Accent: sky blue (#2B8FD4) — Florida sky, water, openness
- Warm highlight: sandy beige (#C9A96E) — South Florida warmth
Emotional intent: trustworthy, established, local expertise

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed hero with overlay text on left, photo right (split)
- Services in a 3-column card grid with icon accents
- Testimonials/stats in a dark navy band
- Contact form with map integration

**Signature Elements:**
1. Diagonal section dividers (clip-path) between sections
2. Subtle wave/texture motif in section backgrounds
3. License badge and trust indicators in hero

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll with section anchors
- Card hover with subtle lift and border accent
- Form fields with animated label float

**Animation:**
- Hero text slides in from left on load
- Cards fade-up staggered on scroll
- Counter animation for stats (12 years, 107 projects)

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (700) — authority, prestige
- Body: DM Sans (400/500) — clean, modern readability
- Labels: DM Sans (600, uppercase, small caps)

## Idea C — Structural Craft Manifesto

**Design Movement:** Swiss Grid Modernism + Construction Vernacular

**Core Principles:**
1. Grid-based precision reflecting engineering discipline
2. Bold typographic hierarchy as the primary visual element
3. Photography treated as documentary evidence of craft
4. Minimal decoration — structure IS the design

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: off-white (#FAFAF8) and concrete gray (#E8E6E1)
- Primary: dark slate (#2C3E50) — solidity, permanence
- Accent: construction yellow (#F5A623) — visibility, action
- Text: near-black (#1C1C1C)
Emotional intent: precision, reliability, no-nonsense expertise

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Vertical rhythm with strict baseline grid
- Hero: large left-aligned headline, right photo column
- Services: horizontal rule-divided list with hover expansion
- Projects: masonry photo grid
- Footer: full-width dark band

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin rule lines as structural dividers
2. Large typographic section headers (oversized, clipped)
3. Photo captions with permit/project data

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Minimal animation — purposeful only
- Accordion-style service expansion
- Sticky header with progress indicator

**Animation:**
- Subtle fade-in on scroll (no bounce, no slide)
- Hover: underline draw animation on links
- Stats: number count-up on viewport entry

**Typography System:**
- Display: Oswald (700) — strong, condensed, authoritative
- Body: Libre Baskerville (400) — editorial, trustworthy
- UI: Oswald (400/500) for navigation and labels

## Selected Approach: **Idea A — Industrial Brutalist Precision**

Chosen for its alignment with the concrete industry's raw material identity. The construction orange accent, concrete texture, and bold editorial typography will make the site feel authentic to the trade while remaining professional and modern.
