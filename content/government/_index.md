## Digital modernization for the public sector

We partner with local and state agencies to replace aging, paper-heavy workflows with secure, compliant online systems. Our solutions help operations, procurement, and community services teams deliver better outcomes with less manual effort.

### Services
- **Government Portals:** procurement portals, permit and application portals, citizen-facing web apps
- **Workflow Automation:** forms, approvals, notifications, reporting
- **Integrations & Data:** secure connectors with CRM/ERP systems, payments, GIS mapping, document management
- **Cloud Migration & Hosting:** reliable, compliant infrastructure
- **Modernization Strategy:** end-to-end planning for legacy system replacement

### Why government agencies choose us

- Compliance-ready, secure infrastructure
- Scalable systems designed for long-term maintainability
- Mission-driven execution with clear documentation
- Rapid modernization capabilities

For federal opportunities, we collaborate with larger prime contractors as a subcontractor.
