# Miami Vice Customs — Design Ideas

## Response 1

<text>
**Design Movement:** Neo-Noir Retrowave

**Core Principles:**
1. Dark, cinematic atmosphere with neon accents that feel like a Miami night
2. Asymmetric layouts with diagonal cuts and angled section dividers
3. High contrast between deep blacks and electric teal/hot pink
4. Typography as visual art — oversized display text used structurally

**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#0d0d0d) base with electric teal (#00e5cc) as primary accent and hot pink (#ff2d78) as secondary. The palette evokes the 1980s Miami Vice TV show — luxury, speed, and South Beach nightlife. Teal signals precision and technology; pink signals boldness and attitude.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed hero with diagonal clip-path divider. Services in a staggered card grid (alternating left-right). Gallery in a masonry-style horizontal scroll. No centered grid monotony.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Neon glow text-shadow on headings (teal glow)
2. Diagonal stripe accent lines (thin, neon-colored) used as decorative separators
3. Car silhouette SVG watermark in hero background

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal neon glow borders on cards. Smooth scroll with parallax on hero. Buttons have a neon pulse animation on hover.

**Animation:** Entrance animations using framer-motion — sections fade+slide up on scroll. Hero text has a staggered letter reveal. Service cards have a subtle tilt on hover.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Bebas Neue" — condensed, bold, industrial
- Body: "DM Sans" — clean, modern, readable
- Accent labels: "Rajdhani" — technical feel for service tags
</text>

## Response 2
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Industrial Brutalism meets Miami Luxury

**Core Principles:**
1. Raw, heavy typography with extreme weight contrast
2. Grid-breaking layouts with overlapping elements
3. Monochromatic base (near-black) punctuated by a single vivid accent
4. Photography-first design — cars as hero art

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#111111) with a single vivid accent of Miami teal (#06d6a0). White used sparingly for maximum contrast. No gradients — flat, bold, confident.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width sections with offset text blocks. Service section uses a numbered list with large numerals as structural elements. Gallery uses a 3-column asymmetric photo grid.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03) in muted tones as background texture
2. Thick horizontal rule lines in accent color
3. Bold all-caps section labels with wide letter-spacing

**Interaction Philosophy:** Minimal animation — only purposeful transitions. Hover states use color fills rather than glow effects.

**Animation:** Subtle fade-in on scroll. No bounce or spring — everything is precise and controlled.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Anton" — heavy, condensed
- Body: "Space Grotesk" — geometric, technical
</text>

## Response 3
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Dark Retrowave Luxury

**Core Principles:**
1. Cinematic dark background with vibrant neon gradients (teal-to-pink)
2. Layered depth using glassmorphism cards over dark backgrounds
3. Diagonal geometry and angular shapes throughout
4. Motion as a brand statement — everything feels alive

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy-black (#080c14) base. Primary gradient: teal (#00d4ff) → pink (#ff2d78). This gradient is the brand signature — used on buttons, borders, and headings. Evokes Miami sunsets, speed, and luxury simultaneously.

**Layout Paradigm:** Hero with full-bleed car image + gradient overlay. Services in a horizontal scrolling carousel on mobile, 3-col grid on desktop. Testimonials in glassmorphism cards. Contact section with a split layout (form left, info right).

**Signature Elements:**
1. Gradient border glow on cards (teal-to-pink border)
2. Scanline texture overlay on hero (subtle CRT effect for retrowave feel)
3. Animated gradient text on key headings

**Interaction Philosophy:** Cards lift with shadow on hover. Buttons have gradient fill that shifts on hover. Smooth scroll with section snap.

**Animation:** Hero has a subtle Ken Burns effect on the background image. Section entrances use framer-motion with staggered children. Gradient text animates its position.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Bebas Neue" — condensed, bold, Miami attitude
- Body: "DM Sans" — clean and readable
- Accent: uppercase tracking-widest for labels
</text>

## Selected Approach: Response 3 — Dark Retrowave Luxury

Deep navy-black base with a teal-to-pink gradient as the brand signature. Glassmorphism service cards, diagonal geometry, animated gradient text, and a cinematic hero with a full-bleed car image. Bebas Neue for display, DM Sans for body. This approach best captures the Miami Vice brand identity — bold, luxurious, and visually striking.
